Books

1. The Two-dimensional Riemann Problem in Gas Dynamics (Coauthored with Tong Zhang, Shuli Yang),  Pitmann Monographs and Surveys in Pure and Applied Mathematics 98, 312 pages, Longman Scientific & Technical, Harlow.

（Math. Review MR1697999 (2000d:76106)：More recently, the two-dimensional Riemann problem has attracted the attention of many researchers, particularly the Chinese school of mathematics: J. Li, D. Tan, S. Yang, T. Zhang, Y. Zheng, etc……A complete and rigorous study is presented for both the two-dimensional scalar conservation laws and the zero-pressure gas dynamics model. Many important properties of the structure and qualitative behavior of solutions are derived for the two-dimensional compressible Euler system. Precise conjectures are stated, which are carefully tested in numerical experiments.）
